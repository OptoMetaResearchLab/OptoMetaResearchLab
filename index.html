Welcome

Welcome to my homepage. Our research focuses on light manipulation, modulation, and its dynamics using innovative systems based on emerging materials like high-index topological insulators and phase-change materials. We harness unique structures such as curved-space metallic systems and metasurfaces to advance light control at nano- and microscales.

Our work explores key applications including nanoscale waveguiding, deflection (prisms), focusing, and beam steering, with prospective projects developing compact solutions for advanced imaging and sensing.
