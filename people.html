Principal Investigator

Dr. Danveer Singh

Principal Investigator

Assistant Professor, Department of Physics, IIT Ropar. Our group works at the interface of nanophotonics, plasmonics, metasurfaces, and advanced optical materials — with applications in sensing, imaging, and photonic device engineering. Recent projects include temperature-invariant plasmonic Bragg reflectors, reconfigurable terahertz metasurfaces, and high-index dielectric platforms for mid-infrared photonics.

Awards: INSPIRE (SHE) Fellowship (2007); INSPIRE (AORC) Fellowship (2013); multiple academic recognitions — see CV.
